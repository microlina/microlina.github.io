Hello!

My name is Catarina and I'm passionate about everything in life.

I was a researcher at NOVA LINCS. I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from Universidade NOVA de Lisboa in 2019. I was advised by Miguel Goulão and João Araújo.

During my PhD journey, I was a Visiting Research Student at SEGAL Research Group, University of Victoria (Canada), working with Daniela Damian. I was also a Visiting Research Student at Bristol Cyber Security group, University of Bristol (United Kingdom), working with Awais Rashid and Ben Shreeve.
