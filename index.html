Short Bio


Hello! My name is Catarina and I'm a Computer Science Ph.D. student at NOVA LINCS, Universidade NOVA de Lisboa, advised by Miguel Goulão and João Araújo.

I received my MSc degree in Computer Science from Universidade NOVA de Lisboa in 2014. The thesis, titled Avaliação da Qualidade em Modelos de Requisitos i*, is available online (in Portuguese).

My main research interests are in the area of software engineering, especially software requirements (elicitation, analysis, specification, and validation), software design, experimental software engineering and software quality.
