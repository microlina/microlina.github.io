Short Bio

Hello! My name is Catarina and I'm a Computer Science Ph.D. student at NOVA LINCS, Universidade NOVA de Lisboa (Portugal), advised by Miguel Goulão and João Araújo. I'm currently a Visiting Research Student at SEGAL Research Group, University of Victoria (Canada), working with Daniela Damian.

I received my MSc degree in Computer Science from Universidade NOVA de Lisboa in 2014. The thesis, titled Avaliação da Qualidade em Modelos de Requisitos i* (Quality Evaluation of i* Requirements Models), is available online (in Portuguese only).

My main research interests are in the area of software engineering, especially software requirements, software design, experimental software engineering and software quality.
