NASA Task Load Index

The following assessment is used to measure your personal opinion on how much workload was required of you during the task you just completed.

In this assessment, you will first be asked to rate 6 workload measures.

After you have completed the ratings, you will be asked to compare which of two workload measure is more important than the other when considering the task you just completed. You will be asked to answer 15 of these pairings.

There is no right or wrong answer.
